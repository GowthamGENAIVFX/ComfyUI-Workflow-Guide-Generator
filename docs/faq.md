
# Frequently Asked Questions

## What is Workflow Guide Generator?

Workflow Guide Generator is a ComfyUI extension that automatically analyzes workflows and generates artist-friendly documentation directly inside ComfyUI.

---

## Does it modify my workflow?

No.

The extension only reads workflow information and generates documentation.

No workflow nodes are modified.

---

## Does it require an internet connection?

No.

Everything runs locally inside ComfyUI.

---

## What workflow types are supported?

Current support includes:

* Flux
* Stable Diffusion
* ControlNet
* Inpainting
* Upscaling
* Video Processing
* Video to EXR

---

## Does it support custom nodes?

Yes.

Custom nodes are analyzed and included in workflow documentation.

Unknown nodes receive generic descriptions.

---

## Is workflow data uploaded anywhere?

No.

All analysis happens locally.

No workflow information leaves your machine.

---

## Can I use it in production environments?

Yes.

The extension is designed for artists, technical artists, and production teams.

---

## Can documentation be exported?

Not yet.

Documentation export is planned for future releases.
